# Contact & Support

Second Stage runs the integration end-to-end and is on hand for any question that comes up — before, during, and after launch.

## Support hours

- **Monday to Friday**, 09:00–18:00 CET (excluding public holidays in Berlin).
- We respond within **24 hours** on business days.

## How to reach us

- **Email** — [hello@secondstage.io](mailto:hello@secondstage.io) for general questions, scoping a new game, or escalating an integration issue.
- **Contact form** — [secondstage.io/contact](https://secondstage.io/contact/){:target="_blank"} if you'd rather use the web form.
- **Onboarding & integration plan** — every active integration has a personalized integration plan and a direct line to the Second Stage team. See [What to Expect](../overview/what-to-expect.md) for what's included.
