# Further Reading

Two ways to go deeper: structured **learning paths** in the Second Stage Academy (free, no signup required) for end-to-end coverage of a topic, and individual **deep-dive articles** for specific questions. Pick the route that matches how you want to read.

## Learning paths

The Academy is curated by the Second Stage marketing team and covers the same concepts the docs reference — but at a tutorial pace, with worked examples and quizzes. Three paths are most relevant if you're integrating or operating TRACKS.
